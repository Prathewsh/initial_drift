# Release Notes - Initial Drift

## [v0.0.1] - 2026-02-27 (Alpha Release)

### Added
- **Core Engine**: Built on Three.js with a low-poly stylized aesthetic.
- **Toon Drift Physics**: Custom physics system focusing on "heavy" drifting and momentum.
- **Dynamic Camera**: Forza/GTA inspired chase camera that reacts to drift angles and speed.
- **Open World**: Initial city layout with procedural building placement and collisions.
- **VFX**: Smoke particle system for tire burnouts and drifts.
- **Audio**: Eurobeat music system featuring "Killing My Love".
- **Controls**: Full keyboard support (WASD + Space).
- **HUD**: Dynamic speedometer and cruise control indicators.
- **UI**: Main menu, pause menu, and "How to Play" guide.

### Planned Improvements
- More car models and customization.
- Expanded map with more varied environments.
- Day/Night cycle and dynamic weather.
- Sound effects (Engine revs, tire squeals).
- Race modes and time trials.
